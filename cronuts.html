Our Craft

What Goes Into Every Cronut

01 48hr

Laminated Croissant Dough

The dough undergoes a 48-hour lamination process — layers of premium butter folded in across multiple cold rests — before being shaped and proofed. The result is the signature flaky-yet-tender interior that distinguishes a proper cronut from a fried croissant shortcut.

02 12

Flavours Across Two Tiers

Seven classic glazed cronuts — from cinnamon through to salted caramel and cookies & cream — plus five gourmet editions finished with premium confectionery toppings. Every flavour is available individually or by the box for café display.

03 AM

Fried and Finished the Same Morning

Cronuts are fried, glazed, and topped on the day of delivery. Unlike reheated product, this means your display cabinet receives pastry at peak crispness — layers still distinct, glaze still set, toppings still intact on arrival.
