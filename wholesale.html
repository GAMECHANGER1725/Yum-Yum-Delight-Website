FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our wholesale program, ordering, and delivery.

What is the minimum order?

Our minimum order value is $50 per delivery. This applies per order regardless of how many product lines you include. There's no maximum — order as much as your business needs.

How do I place an order?

Email your order to info.yumyumdelight@gmail.com with the products, sizes, and quantities you need. For recurring orders, we can set up a regular schedule tailored to your business. Online ordering via Ordermentum is coming soon.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver across greater Sydney, New South Wales. We are based in Western Sydney and service the metro area and surrounding suburbs Sunday through Friday. Contact us if you're unsure whether your location is in our delivery zone.

Are your prices GST-inclusive?

Yes. All prices shown on our Products page are GST-inclusive wholesale prices. Delivery charges, if applicable, are added separately based on your location. Prices are subject to change — we'll notify you in advance of any adjustments.

Can distributors order in bulk?

Absolutely. We work with distributors as well as individual businesses. If you're looking to place large or recurring bulk orders, get in touch directly to discuss volume pricing and scheduling options that work for your distribution model.

Do you offer custom or seasonal products?

Our standard range covers seven categories with multiple flavour options per category. For seasonal items or special runs, reach out via email and we'll advise on availability. Our Gourmet Cronut line is updated periodically with new flavours.
