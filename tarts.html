Our Craft

What Goes Into Every Tart

01 Blind

Stone-Baked Shortcrust Shells

Every shell is blind-baked on a stone deck until it reaches a uniform golden finish. This locks in structural rigidity so the pastry stays crisp under refrigeration — no soggy bases in your display cabinet, even through the afternoon trade.

02 9

Distinct Varieties, One Standard

From tart lemon curd and rich dark chocolate ganache to delicate fresh fruit and velvety custard — every variety is made to the same exacting weight and fill specification so your café receives consistent product on every delivery.

03 AM

Same-Day Fresh Delivery

Tarts are filled and finished the morning of delivery — never held overnight in a filled state. What arrives in the box is what your customers pick up at the counter, at peak texture and flavour.
