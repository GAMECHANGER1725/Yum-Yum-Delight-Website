Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a small Western Sydney operation to a trusted supplier for cafés and distributors across the city — here's the Yum Yum Delight story.

2021
Yum Yum Delight is founded

Launched in November 2021 in Western Sydney with a clear mission: supply premium pastry to B2B customers at prices the wholesale market genuinely needed.

2022
Building the core range

Developed our flagship categories — croissants, tarts, danish, and muffins — refining flavours and sizing options in response to early customer feedback.

2023
Cronuts, Tella Balls & Gourmet line

Introduced Cronuts and Tella Balls to meet growing demand for indulgent options. The Gourmet Cronut line (Snickers, Ferrero, Kit Kat, M&M) launched to an enthusiastic reception.

2024
Sydney-wide distribution growth

Expanded delivery coverage to reach cafés and distributors across greater Sydney, six days a week — Sunday through Friday.

2026
Website launch & digital ordering

Launched our new website to make our full product range and wholesale pricing accessible online. Ordermentum integration for streamlined online ordering coming soon.
