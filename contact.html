Wholesale FAQ Wholesale FAQ
How do I place a wholesale order?

Email your order to info.yumyumdelight@gmail.com with the products, quantities, and your delivery address. For your first order, include your business name and contact details. We'll confirm your order and delivery window by return email.

What's the minimum order to qualify for delivery?

The minimum order value is $50 per delivery. You can mix products across any category to reach this threshold. There's no cap on order size.

Do you supply distributors as well as cafés?

Yes. We supply both individual cafés and food distributors. If you're a distributor looking to add premium wholesale pastry to your range, get in touch to discuss volume pricing and scheduling options.

When do you deliver, and how far in advance should I order?

We deliver Sunday through Friday across greater Sydney. For next-day delivery, place your order by the evening prior. Saturday delivery is not currently available. Contact us if you need a recurring delivery schedule set up.

Where can I find your full product range and pricing?

Our full catalogue with wholesale GST-inclusive pricing is available on the Products page. It covers all seven categories: Croissants, Muffins, Tarts, Danish, Tella Balls, Cronuts, and Gourmet Cronuts. Prices are subject to change — we'll notify you in advance of any adjustments.
