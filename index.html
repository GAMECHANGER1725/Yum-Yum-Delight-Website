The Yum Yum Delight difference

Why cafés choose us over other bakeries.

Since November 2021, we've been baking fresh for Sydney cafés and distributors. No shortcuts, no cutting corners — just consistent product on your counter, six days a week.

About our business
01
Consistent quality at wholesale prices
We make croissants, muffins, tarts, danish, cronuts, tella balls, and gourmet cronuts to a standard your customers will notice — and at margins your business can actually work with. No inflated pricing, no lock-in contracts.
02
Six-day delivery across greater Sydney
Sunday through Friday. Order the evening before, delivered fresh next morning. No bulk lead times, no over-ordering to cover gaps. $50 minimum order per delivery keeps it practical.
03
Full pastry range, one supplier
Seven product categories, all on a single invoice. Croissants to gourmet cronuts — stock your full display case without managing multiple suppliers, delivery windows, and MOQs.
04
Quality ingredients, Sydney-made
Everything is baked fresh in Western Sydney using quality ingredients. No preservatives to extend shelf life — what we deliver is made to be sold that day, the way fresh pastries should be.
05
B2B only — we don't undercut you
We supply cafés and distributors exclusively. We don't sell direct to consumers, so your wholesale pricing is never competing against our own retail. Your margin is protected.
06
Reliable supply since 2021
Established in November 2021, we've built a reputation across Sydney for showing up consistently. Cafés and distributors stay because the product is reliable, the pricing is fair, and ordering is simple — just email your list.
